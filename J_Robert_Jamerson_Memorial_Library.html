Robert Jamerson, a prominent Appomattox resident whose legacy shaped the community. The institution provides comprehensive library services including book lending, computer access, internet connectivity, and an array of community programs designed to serve patrons of all ages. As one of the primary public institutions in the county, the Jamerson Library plays a vital role in literacy promotion, lifelong learning, and community engagement.
